Specialty Repair

Specialty Window & Door Repair

Heavy sliding systems, lift-and-slide doors, and European hardware need a different level of diagnosis than a standard window repair. We handle roller replacement, track alignment, hardware service, and European and Albertini window and door repair.

What We Repair

Specialty Repair Coverage

Lift-and-Slide Door Repair

Diagnosis and repair of lift-and-slide door systems, including operation, alignment, and hardware issues on heavy panel doors.

Roller Replacement

Lift-and-slide and standard sliding-door roller replacement to restore smooth, reliable operation, subject to inspection and part availability.

Heavy Sliding Glass Door Repair

Repair for large and heavy sliding glass door systems that stick, drag, bind, or have come partially off their track.

Track Repair & Inspection

Track cleaning, inspection, and repair, plus panel alignment when a shifted frame or worn track is the underlying cause of a sticking door.

Hardware & Locking Systems

Handle, hinge, and multi-point locking hardware repair and replacement on both domestic and specialty door systems.

European & Albertini Repair

Repair and service for European window and door systems, including existing Albertini windows and doors installed by us or another company.

Glass & Seal Replacement

Foggy or broken glass replacement and weatherstripping and seal service to stop drafts and water intrusion.

Large-Opening Screen Repair

Inspection and repair of retractable, pull-down, and sliding screen systems on large openings, including housings, tracks, and mesh.

Repair options depend on inspection findings and part availability, particularly on older or discontinued specialty and European systems. We’ll always give you a clear diagnosis and honest options before any work begins.
